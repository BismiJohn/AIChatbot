Karnataka gramin bank offers a range of deposit schemes including Savings, Current, Kamadhenu, Fixed, and Recurring Deposits. The minimum deposit amount is Rs 100/- with no maximum limit (PAN is required for transactions above Rs 50000/-). Minimum balance requirements are Rs 0 for no-frill SB accounts, Rs 500/- for general SB accounts, and Rs 1000/- for Current accounts.
